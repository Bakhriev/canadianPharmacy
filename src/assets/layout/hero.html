In 2010, nearly 30,000 pharmacies were operating in Canada, generating a turnover of 50 billion dollars. Although the pharmaceutical retail trade has traditionally been dynamic, its growth rate has recently declined. This is primarily due to a decrease in prices for reimbursable medicines. However, the commercial margin rate, a specialized term indicating the difference between revenue and the cost of goods sold, has been on the rise since 2010 and reached 57% in 2023.

The e-pharmacy sector is constantly changing, influenced by ethical considerations and advertising disputes.
